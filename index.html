Our Plan: Intelligence, Investment, and Integration

  • Intelligence fuses reports, open data, doctrine, and analysis to spot U.S. gaps—like mobility boosts or defense hardening.
  • Investment publishes insights in a DoD-funded marketplace (e.g., $500K prototypes); startups innovate, VC amplifies. Outputs: Proven gear countering adversary weaknesses.
  • Integration aligns with Planning, Programming, Budgeting, and Execution cycles (e.g., Pacific Deterrence Initiative for Indo-Pacific strength against PRC)—ensuring smooth adoption.

Let’s prove it. Together we should start with a test case; run the Intelligence, Investment, and Integration process, iterate on issues we identify along the way, and engineer a competitive advantage beyond China's reach.
