The Problem: Fragmented Playbooks

Despite abundant talent, the defense acquisition ecosystem is fragmented, with players using their own playbooks. This leaves venture capital and startup companies sidelined, confused about their role, like a bad version of "Who's on First?"

The Plan: A Three-Part Process

Synthesize: Combine POM and available intelligence to articulate clear, unclassified technology demand signals to industry.

Engage: Pull venture capital's billions and startup talent into the defense acquisition game.

Sync: Align with government acquisition processes to ensure seamless transition of solutions.
